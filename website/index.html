Our Story

Operation P.E.A.C.C.E. Robotics was founded in 2010 by four friends who were interested in FIRST Robotics at the high school level. Each founder came from a different High School, and as none of their schools offered an FRC program, they decided to approach Litchfield County 4-H to create their own, independent, FRC team.

What is FIRST

FIRST is an organization created to inspire kids to get involved in robotics STEM. There are several different programs in FIRST, each catering to a different age group: FIRST Lego League Jr. (K-grade 4), FIRST Lego League (4-8), FIRST Tech Challenge (7-12), and FIRST Robotics Competition (9-12). Our team is part of the FIRST Robotics Competition (or FRC).
