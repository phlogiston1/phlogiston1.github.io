Music Studio At Tunxis

A project to foster creativity and community.

A Creative Space

Our music studio at Tunxis Community College would serve as a creative outlet for students to express themselves. Supplying students with the same equipment as professional musicians would allow music education at Tunxis to be free from technical limitations.

Open To All

Our studio would be open to all students and faculty of Tunxis, rather than limiting access to only students pursuing a music degree. This would allow the studio to benefit students who are interested in music but pursuing a different degree.

Improving Student Life

The studio would be a campus attraction by forming a community of talented artists to raise each other up. Creating music has been found to be beneficial to people's mental health, so the studio could even help improve academic performance!
